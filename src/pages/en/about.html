About Us

About Udda

"Udda" is an Arabic website that aims to provide a comprehensive collection of free tools and calculators that every Arabic-speaking user needs in their daily life. We believe that useful digital tools should be accessible to everyone, with elegant design, easy user experience, and authentic Arabic language.

Our Vision

To be the leading reference for free digital tools in the Arab world, providing high-quality tools that respect the uniqueness of Arabic and Islamic culture and meet the needs of Arabic users that foreign websites don't adequately cover.

What We Offer

"Udda" includes a diverse range of tools:

  • Calculators: Mathematical, financial, health, religious, and other calculators
  • Converters: Unit, currency, and date converters
  • Text Tools: Tools for Arabic text processing
  • Time & Date Tools: Hijri and Gregorian calendar tools
  • And More: We continuously work on adding new and useful tools

What Sets Us Apart

  • Completely Free: All tools are free and available to everyone
  • Arabic First: Designed for Arabic users with proper Arabic language and right-to-left interface
  • Complete Privacy: Our tools run directly in your browser, and we don't store any data you enter in calculators
  • Modern Design: Elegant and easy-to-use interface supporting light and dark modes
  • Unique Tools: We offer tools that respect Arabic and Islamic uniqueness such as Zakat calculators and Hijri calendar

Website Funding

"Udda" funds itself through non-intrusive ads via Google AdSense. This allows us to keep all tools free while continuing to develop new ones.
